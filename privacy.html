Privacy & Data Use

We take your privacy seriously. This page explains what information we collect via the quick quote form and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Required: name and email (used to provide a quote and contact you)
  • Optional: phone, description (used to improve the quality of the quote)
  • We include a hidden honeypot field to help prevent spam submissions.

How we use your data

We use the provided information to prepare and deliver a quote and to contact you about your request. We do not sell your information.

Retention & sharing

Submitted leads are stored in a Google Sheet accessible by site administrators. We may send email notifications to the addresses configured in the site admin settings to respond to leads.

Your rights

You may request deletion or a copy of your data by contacting us at getmoving4u@gmail.com. We will respond to reasonable requests promptly.

For more information or questions about this policy, please contact us at getmoving4u@gmail.com.
